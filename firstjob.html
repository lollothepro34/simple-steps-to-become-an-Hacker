About the job

The Ethical Hacker is a cyber security expert who carries out cyber attacks on networks, IT infrastructures, websites or applications of the organization he works for, to identify and resolve any vulnerabilities and improve security.

The hacker is a person capable of a deep understanding of computer systems and software, and uses that knowledge to somehow subvert that same technology, a "black hat" hacker does so to steal something of value or with others. criminal intent.
